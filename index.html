About This Project

This portfolio documents the development of an AI-powered learning assistant designed to reduce students' over-reliance on AI and promote independent thinking. The project includes:

  • A detailed proposal outlining the research problem and objectives.
  • Weekly updates tracking progress, challenges, and breakthroughs.
  • Meeting logs documenting discussions with my supervisor.
  • Work-in-progress sections showcasing development milestones.

By the end of this project, I aim to develop a fine-tuned AI model that enhances students' cognitive abilities while maintaining ethical AI usage in education.
